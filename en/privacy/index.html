Privacy Policy

Clear privacy,minimal tracking,high protection by default.

This policy explains what information CatSensor collects, why it is collected, and how users in Quebec and Canada can request access, correction, or deletion under Loi 25.

Effective dateMay 5, 2026

Data We Collect

  • If you request early access, we collect the email address you submit voluntarily.
  • We store your language preference locally in your browser so the site can reopen in English or French.
  • Our hosting provider may process basic technical request data such as IP address, browser type, and access logs to keep the site secure and available.

Why We Collect It

  • To manage the CatSensor waitlist and contact you about launch or product availability.
  • To remember your selected language and keep the site functioning correctly.
  • To prevent abuse, diagnose technical issues, and maintain website security.

Cookies And Trackers

  • No advertising, profiling, or analytics cookies are enabled by default on this site.
  • The site currently uses only a functional local storage setting for language preference.
  • If non-essential cookies or tracking tools are added later, they will stay disabled until you give explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

  • Data may be processed by our website hosting provider to serve pages and maintain security logs.
  • If a waitlist or email delivery service is connected later, it will receive only the information needed to manage signups and launch communications.
  • We do not sell personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

Retention Period

  • Waitlist contact details are kept only as long as needed to manage pre-launch communication or until you ask us to delete them.
  • Technical logs are retained for a limited period appropriate to security and operational needs.
  • Functional preference data remains in your browser until you clear it or change the setting.

Access And Deletion Requests

  • You may request access to your personal information, ask for a correction, or request deletion at any time.
  • Send your request to privacy@catsensor.ca and include enough information for us to identify your record.
  • We will answer within the time required by applicable privacy law.

Privacy Contact

  • Privacy Officer: CatSensor
  • Email: privacy@catsensor.ca
  • Region: Quebec, Canada

Transfers Outside Quebec Or Canada

  • We aim to use providers that offer appropriate safeguards for personal information.
  • If a service provider stores or processes data outside Quebec or Canada, the information may become subject to the laws of that jurisdiction.
  • When applicable, we limit access contractually and require reasonable protection measures.
Loi 25

How this site applies privacy by default.

This page is written in plain language and is linked directly from the website. Non-essential tracking is off by default, and any future tracking that needs consent must stay inactive until the user opts in.

Accessible Policy

The policy is available from the public website in both English and French so visitors can review it before sharing information.

Explicit Consent

If analytics, advertising pixels, or similar non-essential tools are introduced, consent must be requested before those technologies are activated.

High Privacy Defaults

By default, the site limits collection to what is necessary for core operation and functional preferences only.
